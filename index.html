About Us

Excelsior Technical Solutions is a woman- and minority-owned, service-disabled veteran-owned small business (SDVOSB) providing cybersecurity-focused IT consulting, engineering, and program management services to Department of Defense and federal clients.

  • IT Infrastructure Engineering & Operations
  • Identity, Credential, & Access Management (ICAM)
  • Systems Engineering & Integration
  • Program & Project Management Office (PMO)
  • Service Desk & IT Support
  • Software Development & Application Integration
  • Knowledge Management & AI-Driven Analysis
  • Mission Support & Logistics

We design secure, efficient, and mission-ready solutions tailored for defense and intelligence operations.
